Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

tdDate: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh's first satellite, the Bangabandhu Satellite-1, was launched on May 12, 2018 from the Kennedy Space Center in the United States. It was the first geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite for Bangladesh, and the first payload launched by a SpaceX Falcon 9 Block 5 rocket. The satellite was named after the first president of Bangladesh, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.
